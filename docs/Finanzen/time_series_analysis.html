Definition

A statistical method for analyzing and modelling data that is ordered in time, often used for forcasting future values based on past observations

Time Series Processes

Def: A mathematical model that describes how a variable evolves over time

  • Random Walk (non-stationary)
  • Mean Reversion (stationary)

Time Series Tests

Def: Statistical procedures used to assess properties of time series, such as stationarity, autocorrelation, or heteroskedasticity

  • ADF Test

Time Series Models

Def: Parametric or algorithmic representations of time series processes, used to fit historical data and forecast future values

Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average (ARIMA)
  • Used to model the variations in the absolute value of a time series
Autoregressive Conditional Heteroskedasticity (ARCH)
  • Used to model the volatility of time series over time
